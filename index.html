💪 Our Services

Comprehensive Physiotherapy Care

From home visits to clinic sessions — expert rehabilitation tailored to your needs

Home Visit Physiotherapy

Expert physiotherapy in the comfort of your home. Ideal for patients with mobility challenges or busy schedules — we bring the clinic to you.

Post-Surgical Rehab at Home

Structured rehabilitation programs after joint replacement, fracture, or spinal surgery — guided recovery in your own home environment.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Specialized care for elderly patients to improve balance, mobility, and strength — reducing fall risk and enhancing quality of life.

Pediatric Physio

Gentle, age-appropriate physiotherapy for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, and musculoskeletal conditions.

Sports Injury Recovery

Rapid, effective treatment for sports-related injuries including sprains, strains, tears, and overuse injuries — get back in the game faster.

Pain Management Therapy

Comprehensive pain management using manual therapy, exercise prescription, and electrotherapy for chronic pain, back pain, and arthritis.
